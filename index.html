Biographical Sketch

I am a Ph.D. student in the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT, CAS) and my advisor is Prof. Zhiyong Liu. Before that, I received a B.S. degree from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013.

Research Interests

  • The energy efficiency and scheduling of data centers
  • Large-scale data analytics, cluster and cloud computing
  • Combinatorial optimization, approximation, and game theory

Projects

  • Green Multi-tenant DC: a general framework (coupling auction mechanism and game theory) for achieving the high energy efficiency in multi-tenant data centers
  • Backbone Energy-saving: designing novel traffic schedule algorithms and network topologies for reducing the network energy consumption

Publications

2015
